What is Yicem?

Our senior project as Bilkent Computer Science students, Yicem is a mobile application that aims to reduce food waste by creating a connection between the cafes and bakeries of university campuses and the students. Through our application, the students can buy the surplus that usually would go to waste for discounted prices from registered establishments. By creating Yicem, our aim is to increase the profitability of businesses, reduce food waste, and provide people with the opportunity to purchase food at a reduced price.
